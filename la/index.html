CU CS AMA Corps

The CU CS AMA ("Ask Me Anything") Corps is a group of CS students who seek to provide guidance and assistance to their fellow CS students. The AMA Corps is part of the CU CS Foundation Program

Volunteers are able to assist with a wide range of topics including:

  • CU CS SDE and VM
  • Linux OS and Tools
  • General CS Concepts
  • Personal CS-related Projects
  • General CS Program Mentorship
