CU CS AMA Volunteering

Interested in becoming an AMA volunteer?

  • Do you like helping your fellow CS students?
  • Are you looking for teaching and leadership experience?
  • Want to help foster a sense of community within the CS Department?

The Department of Computer Science runs a program to provide general assistance to students taking CS courses via regular staffed "office" hours in the CSEL. We are always recruiting for new volunteers to join the AMA ("Ask Me Anything") Corps.

Requirements

Potential volunteers should generally be Juniors or Seniors who have completed the following CS courses (or equivalents):

  • CSCI 1300 (Intro to Programming)
  • CSCI 2270 (Data Structures)
  • CSCI 2400 (Computer Systems)

Potential volunteers should be comfortable answering questions about the aforementioned courses, as well as talking about their general CS experiences, other CS interests, and any other CS knowledge they may have (Linux, web development, etc). Candidates should also be able to spare about two hours per week during a regularly scheduled time to make themselves available in the CSEL.

Apply

Interested candidates should contact Andy Sayler (andrew.sayler@colorado.edu) expressing their interest and briefly outlining their credentials. We will then follow up with applicants via e-mail.
