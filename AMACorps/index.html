CU CS AMA Corps

The CU CS AMA ("Ask Me Anything") Corps is a group of CS volunteers who seek to provide guidance and assistance to their fellow CS students. The AMA Corps is a new CS department effort as of the Fall 2012 semester. It is part of the CU CS Foundation Program

Think you have what it takes to be a AMA Volunteer? We are currently recruiting volunteers for the Spring 2013 Semester! If you're interested, see this page for details and application instructions.

Access

Access to the CSEL requires a card swipe. All CS majors and any student enrolled in a CS course is eligible to access the CSEL. To active your account and card, please see this web site.

Scope

Volunteers are able to assist with a wide range of topics including:

  • General CS Concepts
  • Personal CS-related Projects
  • General CS Program Mentorship

On-duty volunteers will be sitting in the CSEL during the aforementioned hours and will have both signs and name tags indicating their position. Stop by the CSEL and "Ask Us Anything" today!
