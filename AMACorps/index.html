CU CS AMA Corps

The CU CS AMA ("Ask Me Anything") Corps is a group of CS volunteers who seek to provide guidance and assistance to their fellow CS students. The AMA Corps is a new CS department effort as of the Fall 2012 semester. It is part of the CU CS Foundation Program

Fall 2013 Hours

We hold regular staffed office hours in the CSEL (ECCS 128/ECCS 112) where you can "Ask Us Anything" during the following times (Fall 2013 Hours):

  • Tuesday 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM - Andy - ECCS 112B

Access

Access to the CSEL requires a card swipe. All CS majors and any student enrolled in a CS course should have automatic access the CSEL. See csel.cs.colorado.edu for details.

Scope

Volunteers are able to assist with a wide range of topics including:

  • CU CS SDE and VM
  • General CS Concepts
  • Personal CS-related Projects
  • General CS Program Mentorship
