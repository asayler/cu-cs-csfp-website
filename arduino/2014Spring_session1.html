Session 1

This page is part of the CU CSFP Arduino Project.

In this session we will be covering the basics of the pcDuino. This will include going over the capabilities of the board as well as basic programming techniques. We will also be looking at how to interface accessories with the pcDuino.

A video recording of this session can be found on YouTube.
