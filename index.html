CU CS Foundation Support Program

This website hosts information and files related to the University of Colorado Department of Computer Science Foundation Support program. This program aims to provide tools, materials, and systems in support of the Dept. of Computer Science core curriculum. Information related to various components of this effort is provided below.

CS Virtual Machine

For content and information related to the official CU CS development environment and Virtual Machine , see http://moodle.cs.colorado.edu/course/view.php?id=78.

CSEL AMA Corps

For information on the CU CSEL "Ask Me Anything" generalized office hours and mentorship program, see the AMA Corps page.

Lecture Series and Topics

Need extra instruction on tools we use in the CU CS program (Linux, compilers, debuggers, etc)? Curious about CS Topics not covered in the standard CS courses? Want to brush up on your CS background knowledge?

We host and record regular lectures covering these kind of topics and more. For more information, previous videos, and the current schedule, see the topics page.

CS APT Repository

For information on the CU CS APT repository, see http://apt.cs.colorado.edu.
